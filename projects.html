Creepy Crawler

Whimsical scene located 9 miles east of Miles City, Montana off Highway 94-July of 2015- Baby is 16 feet tall and is made of 175 painted plywood panels. The rhino, velociraptor, and bison are all life-size. Sponsored by the Art Club of Custer County District High School and installed on the Muggli Brothers ranch.

Baby and Dresser/Louis Mohana Furniture

Giant dresser and baby installed alongside the drive up to Louis Mohana Furniture in Bourg, Louisiana-2015- 12 feet tall by 16 feet wide.

Lake Tahoe Historical Society

Located on Highway 50 in Meyers, CA-2013- 32 feet wide by 14 feet tall. A depiction of the Celio Family during one of their bi-annual cattle drives.

Siemer Auto

Friendly Service attendant and old Texaco gas station mural created for Siemer Auto in Fremont, Nebraska-2014- 24 feet wide by 10 feet tall. Nostalgic scene reflecting the quality service and vehicles at Siemer Auto.
